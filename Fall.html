Brand Name: Kiehl

Product Name: Original Musk Blend

Original Musk by Kiehl's is a floral, woody, and musk fragrance that automatically makes you want to swing on your favorite, luscious trench coat as the summer days fade. Top notes are Orange Blossom and Bergamot; middle notes are Ylang-Ylang, Lily, Neroli and Rose; base notes are Musk, Tonka Bean and Patchouli.
