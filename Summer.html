Fragrance Sorcerer

Brand Name: Chanel

Product Name: Bleu de Chanel

Bleu de Chanel by Chanel is a cool and woody fragrance for men that's a refreshing choice for the hot summer days. Top notes are Grapefruit, Lemon, Mint and Pink Pepper; middle notes are Ginger, Nutmeg, Jasmine and Iso E Super; base notes are Incense, Vetiver, Cedar, Sandalwood, Patchouli, Labdanum and White Musk.
