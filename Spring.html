Product Name: Luna Sport Rossa; Prada

Luna Rossa Sport by Prada is a Amber Spicy fragrance for men. The sweet aroma is well balanced with spice and ginger to provide a suitable scent as the days become warmer. Top notes are Juniper Berries and Ginger; middle note is Lavender; base notes are Vanilla and Tonka Bean.
